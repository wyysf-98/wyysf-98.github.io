About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at HKUST, advised by Prof. Ping Tan. Prior to this, I earned my Master's and Bachelor's degrees in 2023 and 2020 at the Interdisciplinary Research Center (IRC-SDU) and worked at Visual Computing and Learning Lab (VCL-PKU), where I am honored to be advised by Prof. Baoquan Chen. During my studies, I also had the opportunity to work as a research intern at Tencent AI Lab, worked with Dr. Xuelin Chen and Dr. Jue Wang .
My research lies in 3D Content Generation, and I am also interested in Point Cloud Processing, Efficient Computer Vision, HCI with AR etc. The 3D world we live in is fascinating, capturing, reenacting the world into the virtual world, and interacting with them will bring more fantasies to our life. For that, I am working on the building the virtual world with the help of AI.
