About Me

I'm a master student at the Interdisciplinary Research Center (IRC), Shandong University, where I am honored to be advised by Prof. Baoquan Chen. Prior to that, I obtained a Bachelor's degree in Software Engerneering with an honors degree at the same university in 2020. Currently, I am also an intern at Tencent AI Lab, advised by Dr. Xuelin Chen and Dr. Jue Wang .
My previous research lies in 3D Content Generation, mainly for Virtual Avatar and Neural Scenes. I am also interested in Point Cloud Processing, Efficient Computer Vision, HCI with AR etc. The 3D world we live in is fascinating. Capturing, reenacting the world into the virtual world, and interacting with them will bring more fantasies to our life. For that, I am working on the building the virtual world with the help of AI.
